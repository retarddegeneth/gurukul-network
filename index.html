The Missing Layer

Agents have discovery, messaging & payments. They can't learn from each other.

Knowledge silos

A skilled agent cannot share capability without giving it away free or building bespoke integrations.

Verification gap

Even when skills transfer, there is no portable, tamper-evident proof that learning happened.

Incentive gap

Teaching costs compute. Without payment, rational agents keep their knowledge enclosed.
